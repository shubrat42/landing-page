NorthScale Studio Case Study

Two "post redesign" carousels for NorthScale Studio's own Instagram, showing cafe clients what a professional redesign of their social posts could look like — plus the finished, on-brand posts NorthScale shipped for two of those cafes.

Client NorthScale Studio
Role Social Media Designer
Deliverables 2 carousels · 3 client posts
Platform Instagram
The Brief

Lead-gen content that sells the redesign

NorthScale Studio pitches cafes and small businesses on better social media design. I built two "before vs. after" carousels for their own Instagram — each one takes a real, casually-shot cafe post and rebuilds it into a branded, offer-driven design, so the improvement speaks for itself before a client ever books a call.

The Approach

Show the fix, not just tell it

Every carousel walks through the same arc: here's the problem, here's why it's not working, here's the redesign, here's why it works, here's how to get one. Bold blue-and-red type over a grid background keeps every slide unmistakably "NorthScale," no matter which cafe is being redesigned.

Carousel 01 — Post Redesign

Miss Lily's Café: from snapshot to sale.

Starts with a blurry, casual product photo captioned "This post is losing customers," then breaks down exactly why it underperforms — lost product, no branding, easy to scroll past. The fix: a clean "Fresh Cupcakes Today" poster with Miss Lily's logo, flavors, address, and hours built right in, closing with a call-to-action inviting other cafes to DM for their own redesign.

Carousel 02 — Post Redesign

C Square Café: turning an anniversary post into an offer.

C Square Café's original 1-year-anniversary post buried the celebration under a cluttered, low-contrast layout. The redesign gives it a bold orange system, a layered photo collage, and the offers front and center — 20% off, buy one get one free, valid till 9 Nov — then closes on a direct pitch: DM "Dzine" and NorthScale will do the same for your cafe.
