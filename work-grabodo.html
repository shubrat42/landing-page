Grabodo Case Study

Two educational Instagram carousels for Grabodo — a UI/UX and web-development studio — one breaking down why websites lose visitors, the other showing everything the studio can build.

Client Grabodo
Role Social Media Designer
Deliverables 2 carousels · 12 slides
Platform Instagram
The Brief

A couple of scroll-stopping carousels

Grabodo, a UI/UX and web-development studio, asked for a couple of carousel posts for their Instagram. I designed two complete sets — twelve slides in total — sharing one consistent brand system: a dark grid background, bold condensed headlines, and bright-green highlight words that carry the eye through every slide.

The Approach

One system, two goals

Both carousels use the same visual language so they read as a series, but each has its own job — the first earns attention by teaching, the second turns that attention into enquiries. The design leans on strong hierarchy and real screenshots so every claim feels shown, not just stated.

Carousel 01 — Educational

Why your website loses visitors.

Built around one sharp idea: a website has only a few seconds to earn attention, and its design decides whether visitors stay. Each slide unpacks a single homepage principle — fast load times, a clear headline, strong visual hierarchy, a hero section that instantly makes the pitch, and one focused call-to-action — using real "good vs. bad" website examples so the point lands fast. It closes on a lead-generating slide offering a free homepage audit. The layout practices what it preaches: heavy type and green accents guide the eye exactly where it should go.

Carousel 02 — Services

One studio, every service.

The second set positions Grabodo as an all-in-one studio — "Stop managing 4 vendors, we do it all." It walks through their four core services — Website Development, App Development, Custom CRM Development, and UI/UX + Branding — and pairs each one with a real interface mockup, so the capability is shown rather than just claimed. It keeps the exact same dark-grid brand system as the first carousel for a consistent feel, and ends with a soft call to book a free 30-minute consultation.
