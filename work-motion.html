/ Motion Graphics /

Short animated visuals with real movement.

Simple motion graphics and video edits made for social — built to add energy to a feed without needing a full production setup.

Role Designer & Editor
Timeline 10 days
Tools After Effects, Premiere
Deliverables Short-form video clips
01 — Brief

Static first, then movement

Started from still frames of the key moments, so the motion had a strong composition to build from instead of improvising in the timeline.

02 — Concept

Keeping it lightweight

Planned quick, punchy transitions rather than heavy effects — the goal was motion that reads instantly on a phone screen, not a showreel.

03 — Refine & Deliver

Cut for sound-off viewing

Timed text and motion so the message still lands with the sound off, then exported in the aspect ratios each platform actually needs.

The finished clips.
